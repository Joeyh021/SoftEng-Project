Our philosophy revolves around the following rules of mentoring:

  1. A mentee should always drive the relationship, setting the agenda and approaching the mentor when help is require.
  2. A mentor will always give up time to the mentee.
  3. The mentoring arrangement should be based on themes and topic areas that they require help unblocking to achieve success.
  4. A mentor should always be from a different business area to the mentee.



achieve success.

The act of mentoring is reliant on the relationship between mentor and mentee. Mentea facilitates that relationship and helps maintain it. Mentea ensures mentoring is beneficial and impactful to both mentor and mentee by following the rules of mentoring.
