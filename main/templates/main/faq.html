Frequently Asked Questions

Visit your profile, then head to the topics section and click edit!

Head over to the Mentee dashboard and then to workshops/group events. You'll be able to events here for the topics you're interested in and others. You are free to join other ones aswell if they interest you. Events may have limited space, so all attendance will be first-come first-serve!

If you want to request a workshop/event on a particular topic, click here. Then fill out the form, and available mentors in the respective topic areas will be notified of you're interest.

Head over to your Mentee dashboard. You'll be able to view mentors recommended to you there.

All you need to use Mentea is internet access and a web browser. This can be on your PC, laptop or mobile.

Plans of Action are ways for mentors and mentees to track their progress and ensure they achieve their goals. You can view plans of actions for your mentees by viewing their profiles, which you can find on your Mentor dashboard. For Mentees, you can view your plans of action by viewing the corresponding mentor's profile. You can find your mentors profiles on your Mentee dashboard.

Please feel free to contact us, or leave us feedback using the feedback form.
